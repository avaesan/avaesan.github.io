The history of Raccandy

The first ever Raccandy restaurant opened in 1978, Melbourne Victoria. A golden and fun time for Australia. Today, Raccandy serves over 100 thousand costumers a month across 100+ restaurants.

The founder of Raccandy has stated that she always had a sweet tooth and a love for sweets and candy. She would make her own recipes and share them with her family, friends, and customers. As a child, her family worked in a bakery in a small town. She travelled across the country selling her candy in hope that her parents business would grow. She spent 10 years perfecting and selling her delicious sweets. Now there are over 100+ restaurants across the country.
